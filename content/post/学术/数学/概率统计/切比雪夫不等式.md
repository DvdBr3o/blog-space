$$
P\left\{ \left| X-E(X) \right| \ge \varepsilon \right\} \le \frac{D(X)}{\varepsilon^{2}}
$$

>[!hint] 证明
>
> $$
> \begin{align}
> P\{\lvert X-E(X) \rvert \geq\varepsilon\} & =\int_{\lvert x-\mu \rvert \geq\varepsilon} f(x) \, dx  \\
>  & \leq \int_{\lvert x-\mu \rvert \geq \varepsilon} \frac{\lvert x-\mu \rvert ^{2}}{\varepsilon^{2}}f(x) \, dx \\
>  & \leq \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}  \frac{\lvert x-\mu \rvert ^{2}}{\varepsilon^{2}}f(x) \, dx \\
>  & = \frac{1}{\varepsilon^{2}}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \lvert x-\mu \rvert ^{2}f(x) \, dx \\
>  & =\frac{\sigma^{2}}{\varepsilon^{2}} \\
>  & =\frac{D(X)}{\varepsilon^{2}}
> \end{align}
> $$
