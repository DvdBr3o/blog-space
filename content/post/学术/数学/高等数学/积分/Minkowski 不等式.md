已知
- $p\geq1$
则
$$
\left(\int_{a}^{b} \left|f(x)+g(x)\right|^{p} \, \mathrm{d}x \right)^{\frac{1}{p}} \le \left(\int_{a}^{b} \left|f(x)\right|^{p} \, \mathrm{d}x \right)^{\frac{1}{p}}+\left(\int_{a}^{b} \left|g(x)\right|^{p} \, \mathrm{d}x \right)^{\frac{1}{p}}
$$
