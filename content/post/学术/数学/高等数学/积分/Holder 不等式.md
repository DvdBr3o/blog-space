已知
- $\frac{1}{p}+\frac{1}{q}=1$

则
$$
\left(\int_{a}^{b} \left|f(x)g(x)\right| \, \mathrm{d}x \right) \le \left(\int_{a}^{b} \left|f(x)\right|^{p} \, \mathrm{d}x \right)^{\frac{1}{p}} \left(\int_{a}^{b} \left|g(x)\right|^{q} \, \mathrm{d}x \right)^{\frac{1}{q}}
$$
