## 结论

已知
$$
\left\{ \begin{align}
x & =x(u,v) \\
y & =y(u,v) \\
z & =z(u,v)  \\
\end{align} \right.
$$
令
$$
\left\{ \begin{align}
E & =x_{u}^{2}+y_{u}^{2}+z_{u}^{2} \\
F & =x_{u}x_{v}+y_{u}y_{v}+z_{u}z_{v} \\
G & =x_{v}^{2}+y_{v}^{2}+z_{v}^{2} \\
\end{align} \right.
$$
则
$$
dS=\sqrt{ EG-F^{2} }\,du\,dv
$$
